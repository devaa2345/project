Age of Chivalry and Faith

The Medieval Age, spanning from the 5th to the late 15th century, was a period of significant social, political, and cultural transformation in Europe. This era witnessed the rise of feudalism, the power of the Catholic Church, and the development of distinct architectural styles like Romanesque and Gothic. Knights, castles, and courtly love defined the cultural landscape, while events like the Crusades connected Europe with the wider world.
