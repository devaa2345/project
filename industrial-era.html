The Revolution That Changed Everything

The Industrial Revolution, which began in Britain in the late 18th century, transformed society from agrarian to industrial through technological innovations. Steam power, mechanized manufacturing, and new transportation systems revolutionized production and daily life. This period saw massive urbanization, the growth of factories, and profound social changes that laid the groundwork for the modern world.
