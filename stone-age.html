The Dawn of Humanity

The Stone Age represents the earliest period of human history, characterized by the use of stone tools. This extensive period, spanning approximately 3.4 million years, saw the evolution of early humans from primitive hominids to modern Homo sapiens. During this time, humans developed fundamental skills like tool-making, hunting techniques, and social organization that would form the foundation of all human civilization to follow.

Discovery/Innovation Approximate Time Period
First Stone Tools (Oldowan) 3.3 million years ago
Control of Fire 1.7 million – 400,000 years ago
Hunting Weapons (Spears) 500,000 years ago
Cave Art & Symbolic Expression 70,000 – 40,000 years ago
